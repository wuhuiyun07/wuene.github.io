Dr. Huiyun Wu is an Environmental Engineer specialized for microbial water quality research. Currenting serving as a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Environmental Health Sciences in the School of Public Health & Tropical Medicine at Tulane University, Dr. Wu received a Ph.D. in Environmental Engineering from Michigan State University. Following the doctoral studies, Dr. Wu undertook a two-year Oak Ridge Institute for Science and Education (ORISE) fellowship with the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) in Research Triangle Park, North Carolina. Dr. Wu has worked on multiple interdisciplinary research projects, including water reuse, wastewater-based epidemiology and sanitary sewage overflow survey, environmental metagenomics, microbial source tracking, stormwater management, and microbial water quality modeling. Dr. Huiyun Wu has passed the Fundamentals of Engineering (FE) and Principles and Practice of Engineering (PE) exams in Environmental Engineering and is pursuing a PE license.
